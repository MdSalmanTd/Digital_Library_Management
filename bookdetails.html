About the Author

Steve Krug

Steve Krug is a usability consultant and the author of the best-selling book "Don't Make Me Think," which has become a classic in the field of web usability. With over 20 years of experience, Krug has worked with numerous clients to improve the user experience of their websites and applications. His practical, no-nonsense approach to usability has made him a sought-after speaker and educator in the industry.

Book Details

Overview

"Don't Make Me Think" is a classic book on web usability that emphasizes the importance of creating user-friendly websites. The book provides practical advice and principles for designing intuitive interfaces that require minimal effort from users to navigate and understand. Steve Krug uses humor and real-world examples to illustrate key concepts, making it an engaging read for both beginners and experienced web designers. The second edition includes updated content to reflect changes in web design trends and technologies, ensuring that readers have access to the latest best practices in usability.
